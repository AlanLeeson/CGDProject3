Dark Room

High Concept

Slender meets I spy

Genre

  • Horror
  • Survival
  • Escape the room

Platform

Desktop - Will develop in chrome.

Story

You've barricaded yourself in a box factory. You know there is something lurking in the room and the lights are out. Defend yourself.

Aesthetics

Graphics Style - Dark and haunting with simple shapes and textures.

Sound - limited sound with minor audio cues given from the environment.

Where sound will play:

  • Ominous background music.
  • When the enemy moves (sometimes).
  • When the gun is fired.
  • Random breathing from the player.
  • Menu buttons.

Gameplay

Mechanics

The player holds a flash-light and searches around the room using mouse movement controls. The player cant move but can look all around. The player can hear rustling around the room and will move the flash-light to investigate. The player has the opportunity to use the left mouse button to shoot at the creature. If the player misses, the weapon has a very long reload time so the player has to try to avoid looking at the creature after it has been startled. After reloading, the player has a chance to shoot the creature.

Controls

Mouse Movement - The player uses the mouse to both point the flashlight and shoot the weapons.

Player Learning

The first thing the player needs to learn is to move the mouse. Because this is relatively intuitive, I doubt we need to teach the player this. Next the player learns that pointing the mouse at a specific point will illuminate the surrounding area. Because the game will show a gun as well as a flash-light on the screen, the player can assume that they have to shoot something. Once they explore the room by shining the flash-light in different areas, they will come across the threatening looking creature. The first instinct would be to shoot it so the player will fire the weapon and either experience the end screen or rush into a panic (depending on their aim). If they miss, the creature will rush the player. The player will probably die, and will be prompted with an end game screen and text saying, "The creature doesn't like it when the light is shined on it." From here on, the player knows not to shine the light on the creature for too long. The last thing the player will learn is that if they miss shooting the creature, the gun will reload very slowly. Here the player knows they have another chance to shoot the creature but that they'll have to wait in the dark worrying about looking at the creature for too long as it moves around the room.
